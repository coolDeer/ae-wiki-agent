    - **Step 3 (Agent Development)**: Develop a native agent (integrating tools like "Co-work") within M365 for multi-app orchestration. - **Competitive Approach to Third-Party Agents**:

- **Full-Stack, Pyramid-based Client Offering**: The company is building a comprehensive, tiered offering to defend its enterprise position.
    - **Hardware Layer**: Top-tier clients receive Nvidia's latest GPUs. Mid-market and SMB clients will be transitioned to the company's proprietary "Maia" chips (e.g., Maia 300) for inference to control costs.
    - **Model Layer**: Offers a tiered model strategy: advanced models (like Claude) plus custom, industry-specific vertical models for top-tier clients; standard models (GPT, Claude) for the mid-market; and simplified proprietary models for SMBs.
    - **Platform & Services Layer**: The "AI SaaS Platform" provides an integrated experience, supplemented by customization and re-training services for large clients.
