We saw a pretty notable difference in relative strength between ES and NQ last week with semis bidding to the moon while non-tech flailed around like a fat middle aged dude with four kids trying to get out of the pool (we just went swimming )??‍♂️

Both NQ and ES formed a multi-day balance above the Thursday 4/16 range. After a gap fill reversal on Tuesday, NQ broke this range to the upside on Wednesday. Thursday saw a deep back test of this multi-day balance that risked looking like a failed break. However, ES was coming down for a LBAF of its multi-day balance range at the same time. NQ saw massive upside continuation, and the ES LBAF finally woke it up just a bit to make a new high on Friday in an apparent multi-day balance break to the upside. NQ has found its way to a 2nd extension off its multi-day box, while ES closed Friday just above its box.

# Trading Lower:

As mentioned in the video, a loss of 7177-7182 begins to look like a failed break of the multi-day range. Is it a valid short? Absolutely. Does it mean that it will be a durable top? Not necessarily. We’ve seen a number of failed upside breaks this year, and it is far less damning than a sustained downside break of a multi-day range.

That said, some initial caution is warranted on the long side if there is persistent weakness back below 7177- 7182 and I will at least initially lean short. I want to be careful we don’t just see a quick trap below 7177-7182.

We have seen liquidations crack levels to the downside, but initiative sellers have yet to actually defend a level from below and produce anything resembling a downtrend on a 30-minute chart. I could almost have left 7167- 7169 and 7144.5-7147 off as weekly levels and used as daily levels which might produce a trap below 7177- 7182. I’ll personally use them to gauge weakness vs a trap on the way to 7115.5-7121.

Stronger buyers would step in by 7115.5-7121 or on a quick LBAF to protect Thursday’s massive excess low. If not, and we see firm offers below 7115.5-7121 then Thursday (and last week’s) low comes into play. If we do actually crack last week’s low with continuation - and this is a good opportunity to bring up the difference once again between RTH participants doing so as compared to some ETH gap down - then the weekly timeframe is brought to balance and we have and intermediate time frame trend pause. The short time frames would be in a downtrend (barring it occurring on a huge gap from here that gets bought) and we would be in seek of a daily higher low.

The prior all-time high on an ETH basis was 7043 and on an RTH basis was 7031. Either of these holding to produce a daily higher low would be constructive, and I would still allow for a quick trap to 7012-7019. Risks increase significantly below there, but we must keep in mind (as from the plans on the initial break) that markets are designed to go up and to the right. Sellers made effectively no attempt to defend a daily lower high coming off the low. As unlikely as that was, it is even less likely that bulls will fail to defend some kind of higher low. Keep in mind we’ve rallied nearly 850 in a straight line off the low. We could retrace 50% of that and on that basis alone it would be a constructive pullback. My point is that if and only we actually get a short time frame downtrend, we would have to have back of mind that a daily higher low to get long can come into play. Just two weeks ago the Sunday night low was 6767 and it triggered the ATH breakout. Show me a multi-week balance break to the downside below there and I’ll start to get pretty bearish while keeping in mind the massive bull gap between 6661 and 6780. This is all wildly premature and my point is to illustrate that there is a long way to go before anything meaningfully bearish comes into play.

NQ has been on an absolute tear, driven primarily by semiconductors. As mentioned above, NQ has fired two additional range extensions from its 3-day range and SMH has come into a major extension. The flip side of that is we are in price discovery, so all bets are off. It would be healthy for NQ to pull back at least a tad and consolidate but it doesn’t mean it will happen. Consolidation above 27150-27169 is broadly constructive. It would look pretty gross giving back most of Friday’s move, but if sellers don’t find any traction below Friday’s low then it would be pretty hard to be bearish. I see 27150-27169 as the first major inflection point for the week and it sits 250+ handles (nearly 1%) below Friday’s close. There is nothing to say about trading higher above Friday’s high as long as it sustains. We simply monitor levels to the upside. I have the most confluence at 27569-27585, 27724-27740, and 27869-27898 which is why they have an asterisk and are in bold but price discovery is price discovery.

NQ had three sets of single prints on Friday, two of which are captured at least in part by the 332-360 (monitor for LBAF) and 267-286 (monitor for LBAF) weekly levels. The third set sits in between. Anything less than a move through 267-286 to 150-169 is a snooze for bulls. Until or unless sellers caps 27150-27169 as upside, they have absolutely nothing.

# Trading Lower:

Holding below 27150-27169 and Friday’s 27130 low (if done on an RTH basis) brings the daily timeframe back to balance and NQ would have “stacked balances” at risk of a “trap door” setup a la October 10th. I believe this is premature, but I see early signs of this setting up just be aware. I’ll continue to monitor this, but should it manifest in the future (even weeks or months in the future) it would look like a rapid liquidation through the range of multiple days - just like October 10th of 2025- and would target last week’s low. Keep in mind I spotted the potential for 10/10 in the weekly plan written 10/4 or 10/5 and said we could go higher and we did all week first. I even said the trap door setup could trigger much later on. In that case it was the same week.

There is every reason to believe this is even more premature so please don’t get overly excited just yet. I’ll monitor with the daily and weekly plans moving forward.

There is some demand around 27041-27069 and it includes this past week’s VAH. It is hard to be remotely bearish if this holds and triggers a reclaim of 27150-27169. Accepting back into last week’s value, however, introduces a fresh layer of (admittedly minor) risk. The 26969-26987 level from last week proved a key S/R, we have an open ETH gap effectively there, and it would represent a 0.618 retracement from Thursday’s low to Friday’s high. The Thursday RTH close sits just above the 894-911 level and would represent a 0.618 retrace from last week’s low to high. I’ll add 26840 as an honorable mention level which I’ll have as a daily level as Thursday’s excess sits below there. Until Thursday’s excess is negated (low taken out) and this past week’s low is taken out with continuation I’ll remain skeptical of sellers.

The 26506-26523 range could still serve as a potential trap below this past week’s low, and below there is a back test of the 2025 ETH high which sits in the 26390-26408 range. The RTH 2025 high was 26326 which should stay on your chart, and I would argue the 26258-26291 level is more important as of now as the first potential trappy back test.

I’ll leave it at that for now. Buyers are in full control until proven otherwise. I have less clarity this week in terms of the most obvious spot for buyers to defend on a quick trap lower to preserve trend, but I would say a hold or LBAF of 7115.5-7121. I have even less clarity on NQ just yet.
