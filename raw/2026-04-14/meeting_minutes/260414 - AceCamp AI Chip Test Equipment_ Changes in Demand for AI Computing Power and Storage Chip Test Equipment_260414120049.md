## AI Test Equipment Market Overview and Supply-Demand Dynamics
- **Overall Industry Supply and Demand**
  - Supply Side: The global supply of chip testing machines is currently very tight, largely due to strong demand from the US and Taiwan following the AI boom.
  - Demand Side: Driven by high-end digital circuit chips, with AI chips being a popular new field. The overall demand is very strong.
- **Key Customers and Market Distribution**
  - Global Market Leaders:
    - NVIDIA is the largest customer.
    - AMD is another major customer.
  - China Market Landscape:
    - Ascend (Huawei) is the largest domestic customer with significant volume.

    - The market is currently still dominated by overseas clients.

    - Pingtouge: Another potential growth source, but its wafer supply solution is not yet confirmed.
- **Testing Flow for Domestic Companies (CP vs. FT)**
  - Testing Focus: The focus for sales in China is increasingly on Final Test (FT).

    1. Wafer fabrication at TSMC.
    2. Packaging and Chip Probing (CP) in Taiwan.
    3. Transporting the packaged chips back to mainland China for Final Test (FT).

## Industry Trends and Equipment Management
- **Packaging and Testing Industry Structure**
  - Coexistence of Models: For the next few years, two primary models will coexist:
    1. Integrated Assembly and Test: Companies like Tongfu, JCET, and Shenghe Jinwei handle both packaging and testing. This model has a larger overall scale.
    2. Specialized Pure-Play Test: Companies like Weice focus solely on testing. This model has gained traction due to US-China relations, which encourage packaging in Taiwan and testing in mainland China.
- **Equipment Reconfiguration for AI Testing**
  - Feasibility: It is technically possible to convert a 93K machine used for consumer products to test AI GPUs by replacing the internal boards.
  - Cost-Effectiveness: Reconfiguration is often not cost-effective.
    - It is more economical to buy a new machine if existing capacity is fully utilized.
    - Conversion makes sense only if there is significant idle capacity on non-AI machines.
    - Purchasing a new machine may come with better discounts compared to buying individual boards for an upgrade.
