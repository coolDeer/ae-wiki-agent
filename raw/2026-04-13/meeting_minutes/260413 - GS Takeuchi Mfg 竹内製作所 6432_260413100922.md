**Q: What is the rationale behind the significant price increases in the European market?**
A: The price increases in Europe are driven by several key factors:
1.  **Yen-Denominated Sales & Exchange Rate Table Revision:**
    *   Sales to major European distributors are conducted in Japanese Yen.
    *   The company used a Euro-to-Yen exchange rate table to set JPY prices. This table was not designed for an era where the Euro reached 180 Yen, which allowed distributors to excessively benefit from the weak Yen.
    *   The company has now revised the table by adding a higher tier, effectively increasing the JPY-denominated sales price. This change is presented as a result of sales negotiations, not just a currency effect.
2.  **Price Restoration in the UK:**
    *   Sales in the UK were sluggish in fiscal year 2022/2023, leading the company to offer discounts.
    *   The current price increase includes a "price restoration" component, raising prices back to their previous levels as the market bottoms out.
3.  **Overall Impact:** The combined effect of these price increases for Takeuchi UK and the revised prices for distributors is projected to be just under 5 billion Yen.

**Q: What is the current market condition and outlook for the U.S. and Europe?**
A: The conditions for each market are distinct:
1.  **United States:**
    *   **Excavators:** The outlook is sluggish due to a delayed recovery in the housing market.

    *   **UK, Austria, Netherlands, Germany:** These markets are generally seen as having bottomed out and are on a positive year-on-year trend, not requiring additional discounts. The UK is also undergoing price restoration.
